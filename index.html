Who are we?

Aluminum Souq represents over 60% of bauxite, alumina and aluminium production in Middle East. Since its foundation in 1986, the members of the AlS have been companies engaged in the production of bauxite, alumina, aluminium, the recycling of aluminium, or fabrication of aluminium or as joint venture partners in such.
We have many customers in other countries. Like: Saudi Arabia, UAE, Bahrin, and more.
We have the high quality goods and the best price. Choosing us is the best decision.
