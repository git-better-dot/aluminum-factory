Alucobond

The versatile characteristics of ALUCOBOND products provide for a plethora of applications such as exterior and interior cladding, column covers, canopies, soffits and other facade applications, allowing architects to offer inspiring, creative, and innovative designs while meeting the standards of sustainable planning. Life and safety is paramount in every decision. ALUCOBOND PLUS was developed to help meet stringent IBC Fire Code requirements while allowing architects and designers the flexibility to create their vision. The fire retardant core offers confidence in design while providing flatness, formability, durability and ease of fabrication.
